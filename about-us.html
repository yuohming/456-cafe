Our Story

456° Café was created from the idea of turning intense challenges into cozy connections. Inspired by the spirit of Squid Game’s Players — resilience, hope, and the belief that life is sweeter when shared — our café blends drama with comfort. Instead of games of survival, we bring people together with coffee, pastries, and moments that truly matter.

456° Café was founded in 2025, It began as a small creative project that was inspired by storytelling, cinema, and, of course, coffee. We wanted to create a space that feels immersive — where the thrill of Squid Game’s world meets the warmth of a neighborhood café. With bold designs, game-inspired details, and a cozy atmosphere, 456° Café quickly became a favorite spot for those who crave something a little different from the ordinary.

At 456° Café, we don’t just serve coffee — we serve experiences, connections, and a playful escape from the everyday.
